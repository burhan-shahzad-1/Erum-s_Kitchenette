Homemade Food Business Website
________________________________________
1. Introduction
This report presents an overview of a web-based system developed for a homemade food business. The project belongs to the online food delivery and e-commerce industry, focusing on home-based kitchens that sell homemade frozen and ready-to-cook food products through digital platforms. This sector has grown rapidly due to increasing internet usage and changing lifestyles.
________________________________________
2. Problem Statement
Small-scale food entrepreneurs, especially home-based kitchens, often lack a digital presence and efficient order management systems. While the demand for hygiene and home-style food is rising, sellers who rely solely on social media lack the structured ordering and management features provided by a dedicated e-commerce platform.

________________________________________
3. Objective
The purpose of this project is to design and implement an e-commerce platform that allows customers to conveniently order homemade food products online. The system aims to:
•	Provide home-based kitchens with a professional digital presence.
•	Implement an efficient order management system to support small-scale entrepreneurs.
•	Combine homemade quality with professional online services to improve customer convenience.
________________________________________




4. Scope of our Project
The project covers the development of a responsive web application optimized for different screen sizes, including mobile and tablet interfaces.

•	Target Users: The system is designed for working professionals, students, hostel residents, and families seeking healthy food options.
•	Technical Implementation: The system requires knowledge of web technologies (HTML, CSS, TypeScript, Node.js), database management (MongoDB), and secure payment gateway integration.
•	Compliance: The scope includes adhering to local food safety regulations, secure handling of customer data, and transparent delivery policies.

________________________________________

5. System Features
The proposed web application includes the following key features:

•	Product Catalog: Display of food categories (snacks, frozen meals, desserts, etc.) with individual product pages and search/filter options.

•	Shopping Cart System: Add-to-cart functionality with automatic calculation of the total price.

•	Order and Checkout: Online order placement with multiple payment options such as Cash on Delivery, bank transfers, and mobile wallets.

•	User Account Management: Registration, login, and profile management for addresses and order history tracking.

•	Admin Panel: Tools for the business owner to manage products, update order statuses, and view sales reports.

•	Customer Support: Integration of contact forms, WhatsApp, and an FAQ section.


________________________________________



6. Conclusion
This project aims to develop a user-friendly and reliable web-based system for a homemade food business. By providing online ordering, secure payments, and efficient management tools, the system helps small food entrepreneurs grow their businesses. The website improves customer convenience, increases business visibility, and supports digital transformation in the food industry. In the future, features such as mobile applications, subscription plans, and expanded delivery areas can further enhance the system.
